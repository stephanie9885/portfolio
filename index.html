About Me

I’m an operations professional with a background in healthcare and people systems. My work focuses on building onboarding programs, streamlining hiring workflows, and designing dashboards that track training progress, compliance, and employee engagement.

I bring a systems-first mindset to operational challenges—connecting people, processes, and data to improve clarity and consistency across teams. Whether I’m designing a SharePoint portal or building performance dashboards in Power BI, I aim to create tools that support decision-making and elevate the employee experience.

Improving Onboarding & Hiring Through Process

When onboarding and hiring processes are disorganized, teams feel the impact immediately. I focus on building operational systems that are just as supportive behind the scenes as they are welcoming on the surface.

From designing SharePoint portals and mapping candidate touchpoints to surfacing KPIs that actually reflect team health—I create infrastructure that scales. These dashboards are just one part of my toolkit. I’ve also led onboarding redesigns, developed training content, and created documentation that aligns HR, clinical, and administrative priorities.
