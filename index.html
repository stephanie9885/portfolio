About Me

I’m an operations professional with a background in healthcare and people systems. My work focuses on building onboarding programs, streamlining hiring workflows, and designing dashboards that track training progress, compliance, and employee engagement.

I bring a systems-first mindset to operational challenges—connecting people, processes, and data to improve clarity and consistency across teams. Whether I’m designing a SharePoint portal or mapping candidate touchpoints, I focus on clarity, scale, and data that supports people-first decisions.
